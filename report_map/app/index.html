2018 U.S. Midterm Election Predictions

We forecasted the two-party voting share outcomes for each of the 435 congressional districts and the District of Columbia in the forthcoming 2018 United States House of Representative Elections, leveraging a unique survey dataset of demographic and pyschometric variables and the statistical modeling technique Multilevel Regression and Poststratification. You can explore our predictions here, and visualize the effects of different voter demographic turnouts on the election outcomes.
